DEBRIS - Detection and Exploration for Rescue, Investgation and Survival

An advanced robotic system designed for navigating challenging environments to aid in rescue operations, investigations, and survival scenarios. Equipped with sensors and a camera, DEBRIS gathers crucial data and imagery, which is then transmitted to a central hub for real-time analysis and decision-making.
